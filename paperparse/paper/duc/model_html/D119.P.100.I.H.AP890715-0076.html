[1] The nonpartisan American Academy of Diplomacy rated Joseph Gildenhorn, Bush's nominee for US ambassador to Switzerland, unqualified based on a comparison of his biography and the job's requirements. [2] Gildenhorn and his family have given $230,000 to Republican campaigns. [3] Twenty of Bush's first 29 ambassadorships have gone to large GOP contributors. [4] Democrats complain that choice diplomatic posts are better reserved for Foreign Service professionals. [5] Gildenhorn, 59, is a lawyer, real estate developer, and vice-chairman of the board of directors of the Sovran Bank. [6] Other ambassadorial nominees rated as unqualified include Joseph Zappala (Spain), Melvin Semble (Australia), and Della Newman (New Zealand). [7] All are in real estate.
