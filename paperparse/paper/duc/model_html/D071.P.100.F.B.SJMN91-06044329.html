[1] Bill McFadden, a 32-year-old professional dog handler, knows that the secret to producing champions is just plain, hard work. [2] Bill, assisted by his wife Taffe, puts in 18 hour days grooming and conditioning his charges, and then crisscrosses the country showing them for their owners. [3] McFadden handles an average of 20 to 25 dogs at each show and works around 120 shows a year throughout the U.S. [4] "We finish with close to 100 champions a year," he said. [5] McFadden's latest product, a Kerry blue terrier named Kerrageen's Hotspur with 37 Best in Show wins, is the top in his breed of all time.
