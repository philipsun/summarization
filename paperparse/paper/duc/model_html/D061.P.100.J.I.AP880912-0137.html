[1] The full force of Hurricane Gilbert slammed into Kingston, Jamaica, at noon on Monday. [2] Torrential rain and 115 mph winds severely damaged the city and its airport. [3] No casualties or injuries have been reported. [4] The storm is expected to move lengthwise across the island, dropping as much as 10 inches of rain. [5] Next landfall is expected to be the Cayman Islands, where tourists are attempting to evacuate and residents were making preparations.
