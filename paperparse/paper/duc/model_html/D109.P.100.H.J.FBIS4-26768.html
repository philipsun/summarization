[1] Wen Jiabao (alternate member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and CPC Central Committee Secretariat member) accompanied by Mao Zhiyong (secretary of the Jiangxi Provincial Party Committee) and Governor Guanzheng, inspected Jiangxi province flood areas and issued instructions. [2] We Jiabao expressed appreciation to the servicemembers and civilians fighting the floods. [3] He pointed out, however, that while continuing the work in Ganjiang River valleys, flood-prevention in the Poyang Lake area should be stressed. [4] Further, Jiangxi is a major agricultural province. [5] Immediate action should be taken to salvage the early-ripening rice and expand the planting of late-ripening rice and autumn grain.
