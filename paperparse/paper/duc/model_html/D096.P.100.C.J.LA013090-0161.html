[1] Though 63% of TV viewers or approximately 109 million people watched Super Bowl XXIV on CBS, the network finished the week in the cellar in the ratings race. [2] The lopsided game was the lowest rated Super Bowl since 1969. [3] Each rating point in 1990, however, represents many more households than it did two decades ago. [4] This marks only the third time that the Super Bowl network did not win the week and only the second time that it was third for the week. [5] On the upside, the game was a good lead-in to the new CBS series, "Grand Slam".
