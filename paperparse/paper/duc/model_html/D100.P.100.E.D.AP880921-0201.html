[1] Lennon biographer Albert Goldman has defended his controversial biography. [2] In it he describes the former Beatle as a volatile, debauched recluse, who stayed in bed for days at a time. [3] Family and friends of John Lennon denounced the book, charging the book was fiction. [4] Goldman claims that he is being attacked because his book is bad for business. [5] He says that his sensational charges of bisexuality, drug abuse and wild drinking binges fit together with the image Lennon presented of himself. [6] Goldman conducted 1200 interviews over 6 years while researching the book. [7] Yoko Ono, Lennon's widow did not cooperate with the author.
