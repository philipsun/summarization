[1] President Aquino used small bombers and artillery to end a rebellion led by renegade army Col. Alexander Noble on Mindanao, countering charges that her government is indecisive. [2] The Oct. 4-6 uprising never spread to the military underground on other islands. [3] Aquino has survived two major coup attempts and several aborted military mutinies. [4] Most ended in standoffs or dragged on for days because military leaders were reluctant to order troops to fire on other soldiers. [5] Noble's rebellion coincided with the release of a government report that concluded coup attempts will probably continue -- but unlikely to succeed -- for the indefinite future.
