[1] Lucille Ball, star one of the most popular shows in the history of television "I Love Lucy", died at the age of 77 in April 1989. [2] Miss Ball and her late former husband, Desi Arnaz starred in the show from 1951 to 1957 and it has since been widely syndicated in reruns. [3] Together they formed Desilu Productions which made some of the top comedy shows of the '50s and '60s. [4] The couple divorced in 1960. [5] Following the divorce, Miss Ball married Gary Morton and continued on in her successful show business career. [6] Her last appearance was at the 61st Academy Awards ceremony in March 1989.
