[1] The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences tries to keep Oscar's sheen from being tarnished by commercialism, even though it itself suggested limousines bringing celebrities to the awards have door-mounted coolers bearing the Coca Cola logo. [2] It criticized MGM-UA for touting "Rain Man" nominations before they were made.
