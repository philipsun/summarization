[1] Michael Stoney, a senior executive in a number of Maxwell companies, and Kevin and Ian Maxwell, the sons of the late Robert Maxwell, are named as the three top persons involved in at least 28 "unusual" payments from Mirror Group Newspapers (MGN) bank accounts.
