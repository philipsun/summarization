[1] More than 2,500 dogs, representing 118 breeds, will compete at Sunday's All Breed Dog Show and Obedience Trials at the Los Angeles Sports Arena. [2] They will undergo demonstrations and tests to show their skills. [3] This is a chance for all to learn about the possibilities, advantages and disadvantages of different breeds. [4] Other attractions include an expert who will answer behavioral questions about pets, free veterinary advice, a demonstration of how 4-H youngsters train puppies to become guide dogs and more than 70 vendors selling dog paraphernalia. [5] A computer will enable visitors to determine which breeds would best suit their life styles.
