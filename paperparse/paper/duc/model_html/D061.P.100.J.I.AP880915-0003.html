[1] Hurricane Gilbert slammed into Mexico's Yucatan Peninsula on Wednesday. [2] The eye hit Cancun and Cozumel at 8 a.m. carrying winds up to 160 mph. [3] Heavy flooding, destroyed slums, and severed communications and power lines were reported. [4] No relief efforts were yet underway and bands of youth were said to be looting in Cancun. [5] About 175,00 people had been evacuated from the coast. [6] Gilbert, a Category 5--the deadliest--has already killed 24 people in the Caribbean. [7] It is now moving over the Yucatan toward the Gulf of Mexico where warnings have been issued and ports and airports closed.
