[1] The Tass news agency reports that trucks from Soviet Azerbaijan carrying 100 doctors and earthquake relief supplies reached Iran on Saturday. [2] In Baku, capital of Azerbaijan, a day of mourning was declared. [3] Soviet President Gorbachev in a message to Iranian President Rafsanjani sent his condolences "to the families and friends of those who lost their lives in the earthquake". [4] Iranian officials said they would welcome assistance from all countries except Israel and South Africa. [5] Around the world countries responded generously including France, the European Community, the United States, the Vatican, Holland, Turkey, Greece, Pakistan, Belgium, Finland, India, Bangladesh, Yugoslavia, Sri Lanka and Kuwait.
