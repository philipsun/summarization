[1] A biography of former Beatle John Lennon by Albert Goldman, discloses a sometime unflattering portrait of both Lennon and his second wife Yoko Ono. [2] Goldman claims that Lennon spent much of a three-year span sleeping or smoking marijuana. [3] Between 1976 and 1979 the author says Lennon was a recluse. [4] Lennon also spent most of his adult life as an anorexic. [5] The biography also details Yoko Ono's $5,000-a-week heroin habit in 1979. [6] Goldman spent more than six years researching the book, interviewing 1200 friends, relatives and associates of Lennon, who was murdered in New York in 1980.
