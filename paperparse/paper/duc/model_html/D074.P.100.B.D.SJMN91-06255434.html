[1] Virginia Thomas, wife of Supreme Court nominee Clarence Thomas shares her husband's conservative views. [2] She represented the conservative viewpoint in her jobs as a staffer for a Republican congressman, spokeswoman for the U.S. [3] Chamber of Commerce and deputy assistant secretary at the Department of Labor.
