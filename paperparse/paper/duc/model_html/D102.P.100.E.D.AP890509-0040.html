[1] Lucille Ball, 77, who starred in one of televisions biggest all-time hits, "I Love Lucy", died April 26, 1989 following emergency heart surgery. [2] The show was popular with generations of fans, as a first run show in the 50's and for three decades of reruns. [3] She was eulogized in memorial services throughout the country. [4] Diane Sawyer, Carol Burnett, Rev. Robert Schuller and others praised the red headed comedian who made them laugh. [5] Her husband of more than 20 years, Gary Morton attended a private service for his wife. [6] Her remains were buried at Forest Lawn Memorial Park in Hollywood Hills.
