[1] On Sunday an anti-American newspaper in Iran blamed the United States for the death of tens of thousands of people in last week's earthquake, since it plundered Iran's oil and other resources for 25 years preventing application of the national wealth to development of "advanced (anti-seismic) building construction systems". [2] After its expulsion from Iran, the argument continued, the U.S. imposed economic sanctions and launched plots that halted the renovation of the country. [3] The paper called on Iranians to reject relief offers by the U.S. and "other governments whose hands are stained with the blood of the Iranian people". [4] Iran has said it would welcome relief from U.S. humanitarian groups.
