[1] John Lennon's 50th birthday was observed with a global celebration as his peace anthem "Imagine" which was played for an estimated 1 billion people in 130 countries. [2] The song followed a brief ceremony at the United Nations that featured the former Beatle's wife Yoko Ono and the wife of the U.N. [3] Secretary General. [4] Fans also gathered in Liverpool and New York to pay tribute to Lennon. [5] The tribute coincides with the release of a new collection of Lennon's work. [6] Lennon was gunned down by an obsessed fan in December 1980 outside his Manhattan apartment building.
