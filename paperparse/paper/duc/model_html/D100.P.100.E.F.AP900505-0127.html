[1] Thousands of John Lennon fans attended a benefit concert in his hometown, Liverpool, honoring the late, former Beatle. [2] Proceeds will go to the Spirit Foundation, the charity founded by John and Yoko two years prior to his murder by Mark Chapman in New York. [3] Many musicians from the US and Britain performed at the concert. [4] The other former Beatles did not attend, but Paul McCartney and Ringo Starr sent taped messages. [5] The musicians all assembled on stage for "Give Peace a Chance". [6] The concert concluded with a recording of Lennon singing "Imagine" as giant screens showed a video featuring John and Yoko.
