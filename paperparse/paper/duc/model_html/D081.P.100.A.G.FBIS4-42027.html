[1] In Yenakiyevo in the Donetsk Oblast, miners at six coal production association and pits went on strike demanding payment of wage arrears. [2] A miners' rally was held near the town council building. [3] A strike committee was elected which decided to continue the strike until demands were fulfilled. [4] Shift workers of a mine in Ugledar refused to work for the same reason. [5] The mine's director promised to pay the arrears. [6] Shift workers at two mines in Pervomaysk, Lugansk Oblast, refused to start work for political reasons--to compromise the existing authorities, particularly President Leonid Kravchuk, before the second round of political elections.
