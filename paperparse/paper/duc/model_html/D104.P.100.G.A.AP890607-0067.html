[1] The exact number of people killed during the martial law occupation of Beijing may never be known. [2] One government statement says that 300 soldiers, "thugs," bystanders and students have been killed. [3] Another says that 400 missing soldiers may be dead or wounded. [4] Unofficial estimates of the death toll range from 500 to 12,000 people. [5] Officials of the Chinese Red Cross estimated 2,600 killed while one Hong Kong newspaper said that 5,000 were killed and 30,000 injured. [6] Nearly all of the deaths and injuries occurred Saturday night and early Sunday when troops fired randomly into large crowds while forcing their way to Tiananmen Square.
