[1] Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto further raised tensions by accusing India on Thursday of conducting genocide in Kashmir, killing Islamic scholars and citizens. [2] She was referring to the recent assassination of a top Moslem cleric and the killing of 58 people during his funeral procession. [3] Ms. Bhutto had just returned from a mission to Islamic nations in the Middle East and North Africa. [4] She claims full support from these nations against Hindu-dominated India. [5] Pakistan and India already have fought two wars over Kashmir. [6] Since India began a crackdown against Moslem separatists in January, more than 400 people have been killed.
