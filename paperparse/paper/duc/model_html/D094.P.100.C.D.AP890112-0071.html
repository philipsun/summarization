[1] A British Broadcasting documentary claims that U.S. prosecutors pressured Japan's wartime prime minister into changing testimony at his trial to avoid incriminating Emperor Hirohito in war crimes. [2] General MacArthur feared that if Hirohito were indicted, it would touch off a guerrilla war and the documentary claims the decision not to prosecute was Truman's. [3] The documentary challenges Hirohito's image as a peaceful man, saying he must have know about the massacre of Chinese at Nanking and knew more than a month in advance of Japan's plan to attack Pearl Harbor. [4] Hirohito died in 1989 at the age of 87.
