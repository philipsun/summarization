[1] Joe Montana's 10-yard touchdown pass to John Taylor with 34 seconds left in the game gave the San Francisco 49ers a 20-16 Super Bowl victory over the Cincinnati Bengals on Sunday. [2] The touchdown gave Montana a Super Bowl record with 357 yards passing. [3] Wide receiver Jerry Rice caught 12 passes for 222 yards, both Super Bowl records, and was named the game's most valuable player. [4] It was the third Super Bowl win for 49ers Coach Bill Walsh. [5] It was the fifth straight win for the NFC team and the most dramatic. [6] The previous four had an average score of 41-14.
