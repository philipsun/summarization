[1] Lucille Ball was unusual in the entertainment world in that she never bragged, never exercised her considerable power and seemed astonished when honors came her way. [2] She always credited others for the success she achieved. [3] Miss Ball was late in reaching her comedy potential. [4] She was 40 when she starred in "I Love Lucy" which was to revolutionize television. [5] She rarely indulged in self-analysis, although she did say that she was ambivalent about the last 5 years of the show. [6] Her husband and costar Desi Arnaz was drinking and carousing and it was a terrible embarrassment for her.
