[1] The Indian government has been fighting Muslim insurgents in the state of Jammu- Kashmir for the last seven months. [2] More than 1,000 people have been killed. [3] India (predominately Hindu) and Pakistan (predominately Islamic) have fought two wars over Kashmir since the nations were established in 1947. [4] Jammu-Kashmir is 62 percent Muslim and the insurgents want an independent nation. [5] Violence is occurring primarily near Srinagar, the capital, and along the border with Pakistan. [6] India claims the insurgents are being trained and armed in Pakistan. [7] Pakistan denies these charges. [8] The two nations recently exchanged fire, but both say there is no cause for concern.
