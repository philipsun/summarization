[1] Debi Thomas, the US hope for a figure skating gold medal in the 1988 Winter Olympics, was disappointed when the Gold went to Germany's Katarina Witt and the Silver to Canadian Elizabeth Manley. [2] Manley was loudly cheered by fellow Canadians throughout her performance. [3] Thomas, who was a US and 1986 world champion, was awarded the Bronze medal. [4] Other US figure skating medal winners Brian Boitano with the Gold and Jill Watson and Peter Oppegard with the Bronze in pairs. [5] US speed skaters won gold, silver, and bronze with Bonnie Blair taking both a gold and a Bronze.
