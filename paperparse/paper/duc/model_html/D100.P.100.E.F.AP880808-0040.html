[1] According to biographer Albert Goldman in his book The Lives of John Lennon the late, former Beatle spent much of a three-year period sitting in his room in the lotus position and smoking marijuana. [2] Between 1976 and 1979, Goldman writes that Lennon "could not be more remote". [3] Lennon was an anorexic, the eating disorder traced to a comment that he was "the fat Beatle". [4] The book claims that Lennon's second wife, Yoko Ono, had a $5,000 a week heroin habit and that John also took heroin and consumed LSD. [5] Goldman quotes Lennon as saying about LSD "I just ate it--like candy".
