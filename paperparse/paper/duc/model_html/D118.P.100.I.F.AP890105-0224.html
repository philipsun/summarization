[1] Former Senator John Tower, the defense secretary-designate had surgery for removal of a colon polyp. [2] Preliminary biopsy showed the polyp to be benign. [3] Tests of the growth showed it to contain some "malignant but well differentiated cells". [4] The ex-senator is expected to make a full recovery. [5] Tower went into business as a defense consultant after he left the Senate in 1985, having served there for 24 years. [6] He has also served as an arms negotiator for President Reagan and he headed the White House's Iran-contra investigative panel. [7] President-elect Bush named Tower as his defense secretary on December 16th.
