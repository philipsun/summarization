[1] An explosion this morning destroyed a military barracks that housed the Royal Marines School of Music. [2] The School is located in the southern England town of Deal, and police there said the explosion killed 11 and injured 22. [3] The Irish Republican Army claimed responsibility for the explosion. [4] The blast occurred while the school's neighbors were still in bed, or at home preparing for the day's activities. [5] Some homes in Deal were badly damaged. [6] Neighbors described the horror and confusion that followed the explosion, and likened it to those that occurred during the Blitz.
