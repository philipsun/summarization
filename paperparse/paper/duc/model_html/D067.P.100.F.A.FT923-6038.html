[1] Hurricane Andrew yesterday smashed its way through Louisiana causing heavy damage in rural communities but missing low-lying New Orleans. [2] After hitting Louisiana with winds up to 120 miles per hour, Andrew lost force and by yesterday afternoon was down-graded to a tropical storm. [3] Early reports indicate one death, 75 injuries, and thousands homeless in Louisiana after 14 deaths in Florida and three in the Bahamas. [4] Across Florida 2 million people were without electricity yesterday. [5] There was more than $15 billion property damage in Florida, but most of it was to uninsured property, so insurers' losses are likely to total less than $1 billion.
