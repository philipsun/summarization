[1] Following two weeks of tension caused by Iraqi accusations of Kuwaiti harmful wrongdoing; Iraq invaded and quickly overran Kuwaiti defenses. [2] Kuwait's leaders escaped to Saudi Arabia, however, the emir's brother was killed defending the palace. [3] Iraq's government said it invaded at the request of revolutionaries who had already staged a coup. [4] It was the first time in modern history that one Arab nation invaded another. [5] The al Sabah family has ruled the city-state of Kuwait for almost 250 years. [6] The "provisional government" announced that it was dissolving Kuwait's parliament and would soon hold "free and honest elections".
