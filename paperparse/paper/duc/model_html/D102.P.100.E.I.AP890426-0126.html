[1] Keith Thibodeaux, who played Little Ricky on the "I Love Lucy" show, remembered Lucille Ball on Wednesday, following the announcement of her death. [2] Thibodeaux grew up on the set playing the role from 1956 to 1961. [3] He said he saw more of Lucy than his parents. [4] Thibodeaux is a professional drummer but auditioned for the Ricky role when he was 6. [5] He remembers Lucy saying "that's him" and he was signed to a seven-year contract, using the name Richard Keith. [6] He recalls Lucy as being very serious, in control, and very strict, but with a lot of love and generosity.
