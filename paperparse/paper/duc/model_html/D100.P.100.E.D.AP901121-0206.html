[1] In the decade since the death of former Beatle John Lennon, his wife, Yoko Ono has issued several albums and collections of his work. [2] Ono says she wants to keep Lennon's spirit alive, but others charge that she has made millions with no concern for his legacy. [3] Ono has designed and sold memorabilia from coffee mugs to lithographs, using Lennon's image. [4] Ono says that some of the money goes to the Spirit Foundation, established by Lennon and her before his death. [5] The foundation has contributed to causes ranging from a home for drug-addicted children to bulletproof vests for police officers.
