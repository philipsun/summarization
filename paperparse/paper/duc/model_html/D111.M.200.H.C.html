[7] A 1989 scandal that involved Austrian politicians grew out of the mysterious sinking of the freighter Lucona in the Indian Ocean 12 years earlier. [8] The Ostwind, a yacht built for Hitler, came to an ignominious end off the coast of Florida. [9] In the same month whales attacked and sank the American pleasure boat Sibonay off Costa Rica. [10] In January 1989 an Argentine supply ship sank two miles off the Antarctic Peninsula.
