[1] Fifty-nine people drowned Sunday afternoon when a Caribbean passenger ferry sank while being towed into port by a tugboat. [2] The ferry Justo Rufino Barrios II ran out of fuel midway on its regular 16-mile run across Amatique Bay, from Livingston to Puerto Barrios. [3] Vicente Daudi, a 40-year-old Spanish citizen, was killed and two of his daughters are missing. [4] Daudi was working with a Spanish technical aid and economic development mission in Guatemala. [5] Navy patrol boats, fishing vessels, and private craft have been searching for survivors. [6] Puerto Barrios, 187 miles NE of the capital, is Guatemala's principal east coast port.
