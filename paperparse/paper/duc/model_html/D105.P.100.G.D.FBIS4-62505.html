[1] The Tajik Communist Party has called for the restoration of a renovated Soviet Union in the form of a federation as the only means to ensure the development of the political, economic and spiritual potential of the former Soviet republics. [2] They claim that the disintegration of the Soviet Union and the parade of sovereignty that followed have led to economic and political chaos. [3] Tajikistan was very hard hit by the wave of ethnic conflict and general unrest that occurred when the Soviet Union collapsed. [4] The civil war in the republic claimed over 100,000 lives. [5] The Party says the majority of Tajiks support such a restoration.
