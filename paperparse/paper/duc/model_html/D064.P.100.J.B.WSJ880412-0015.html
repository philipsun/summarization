[1] McDonald's will open its first outlets in Korea through a 50-50 joint venture, McAhn Industries Ltd., founded with Harvard trained accountant-turned-entrepreneur, Mr. [2] Ahn Hyo Young. [3] McAhn struggled to overcome government red tape and to find reliable local produce suppliers. [4] Starting modestly, their strategy is to open three outlets this year and five each year thereafter. [5] South Korea has Asia's fastest growing economy. [6] McAhn's hope is to develop McDonald's image as an affordable place for the Korean middle class rather than a familiar name for foreigners. [7] Attention will be given to attracting children. [8] Currently they face competition from Wendy's, Burger King and homegrown burger chains.
