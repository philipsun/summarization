[1] Thunderstorms swept through eastern Ohio causing flooding that killed at least ten people, left many missing, and drove hundreds from their home. [2] Ohio Governor Celeste declared a state of emergence and surveyed the area. [3] Temporary morgues were set up at the Bellaire City Hospital and at a funeral home in Shadyside, a community of 4,300 about ten miles south of Wheeling, West Virginia. [4] Two creeks in this area that empty into the Ohio River were the main area where flooding occurred. [5] The Ohio Emergency Management Agency was assisted in coping with the emergency by other state agencies and the Red Cross.
