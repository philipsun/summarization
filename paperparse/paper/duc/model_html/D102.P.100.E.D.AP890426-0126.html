[1] The actor who at the age of 6 began playing Little Ricky on the "I Love Lucy" show, Keith Thibodeaux, paid tribute to the show's star, Lucille Ball. [2] Miss Ball, 77, died in Los Angles in April 1989. [3] Thibodeaux, 38 played Little Ricky from 1956 until 1961.
