[1] According to Niranjan Thapa, Nepal's minister of state for home affairs, 21 of Nepal's 75 districts suffered extensive damage in Sunday's earthquake on the India-Nepal border. [2] In Nepal, over 450 were killed and up to 1,000 were injured. [3] Indian officials reported at least 200 dead and over 2,000 injured, most by collapsing buildings.
