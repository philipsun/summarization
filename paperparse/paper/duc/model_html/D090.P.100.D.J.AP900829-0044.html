[1] President Corazon Aquino today reversed a long-held policy and said she was willing to negotiate cease-fires with Communist guerrillas and former soldiers who have staged coup attempts. [2] Mrs. Aquino also invited her most outspoken critic Sen. Juan Enrile to join the talks. [3] Enrile said he was willing to reconcile with Mrs. Aquino, who fired him as defense minister after he was implicated in a coup attempt in November 1986. [4] No one has claimed responsibility for the explosions that occur almost daily in Manila. [5] The Government blames them on Communist rebels who have repeatedly tried to topple Aquino.
