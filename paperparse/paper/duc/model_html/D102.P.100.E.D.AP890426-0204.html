[1] Gale Gordon, a sidekick of Lucille Ball from radio days who appeared with her in three television series recalled how the comedian never thought of herself as particularly funny. [2] Lucille Ball died in April 1989 at the age of 77. [3] Gordon first met Miss Ball in 1947 in the radio program "My Favorite Husband", which later became the television show "I Love Lucy". [4] They starred together in "The Lucy Show" which ran from 1962- 68, in "Here's Lucy" from 1969-1975 and briefly in 1986 in "Life with Lucy". [5] "The world will miss her greatly," Gordon said.
