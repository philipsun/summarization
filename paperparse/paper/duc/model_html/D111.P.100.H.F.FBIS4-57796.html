[1] An Argentine corvette carried out control operations against a Korean vessel illegally fishing in Argentine waters. [2] The Foreign Ministry approved the intervention as being in accordance with priority to be given to preserve marine life resources and punishing violators of national laws. [3] The Korean crew sank its own ship after it failed to answer the Argentine ship's warnings. [4] Another Korean ship nearby rescued the crew of the sinking ship. [5] The incident occurred about 15 nautical miles off the coast of Malvinas so the Argentine ship transferred assistance responsibility to British authorities in accordance with bilateral agreements.
