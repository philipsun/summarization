[1] Expanded and accelerated release programs already have begun for recordings by Leonard Bernstein, one of the most documented conductors in history. [2] Sony Classical will begin its three-year release of 75 CDs in January. [3] Deutsche Grammophon is looking at his discography. [4] They had released a CD set in August called "The Bernstein Edition", which is "selling well". [5] BGM Classics/RCA Red Seal plans a March re-release of Bernstein's first commercial recordings from the 1940's. [6] He had over 200 classical recordings, but only six videos are available in the home market--three 1971 Beethoven programs and three movies with scores he composed.
