[1] According to the State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters, water levels of the Xi Jiang, Bei Jiang, Xiang Jiang, and Gan Jiang rivers are dropping. [2] The Xi Jiang and Bei Jiang, in Guangdong Province, crested yesterday, the Bei Jiang at a record 10.39 meters. [3] The industrialized Zhujiang Delta, however, is still threatened because it is traversed by a merged Xi Jiang and Bei Jiang. [4] The Xiang Jiang crested on 19 June, but today the water level is still at 25 meters. [5] Dike reinforcement, therefore, must be accelerated. [6] The flood peak in Gan Jiang entered Boyang Lake on 20 June.
