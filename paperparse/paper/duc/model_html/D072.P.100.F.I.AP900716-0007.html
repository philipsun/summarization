[1] Conductor Leonard Bernstein has cancelled several performances in Japan due to exhaustion and lingering illness. [2] He will return to the United States to recuperate. [3] His doctor announced he never fully recovered from ailments he suffered in the spring and needs complete rest for an extended period. [4] Bernstein had been scheduled to conduct the London Symphony Orchestra in four performances in Japan and appear with young musicians during the Pacific Music Festival. [5] He inaugurated the festival last month, believed to be the first festival in Asia where young musicians can train with a professional orchestra. [6] Bernstein issued a separate apology to his fans and sponsors.
