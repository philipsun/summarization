[1] Rain in the India-Nepal border region where a major earthquake struck Sunday is hampering rescue efforts. [2] Landslides block many roads, railroad tracks are sunk in mud, and bridges over rain swollen rivers are cracked. [3] Officials in Nepal say more than 497 people died. [4] Authorities in India report 200 deaths in Bihar, but news reports say 400 died. [5] Officials in both countries expect the death toll to climb once reports arrive from remote regions.
