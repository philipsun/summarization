[1] A campaign at Northeastern University to award an honorary degree to South African nationalist leader Nelson Mandela has been successful. [2] It was necessary to change a university policy, which barred honorary degrees for anyone who could not be present at the ceremony. [3] Mandela is imprisoned in South Africa and the white-minority government will free him only if he renounces the use of violence by forces seeking political power for blacks. [4] South African Archbishop Desmond Tutu will accept the degree for Mandela. [5] The only remaining hitch is getting Mandela family approval, which has not yet been received.
