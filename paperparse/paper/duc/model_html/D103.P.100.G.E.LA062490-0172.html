[1] Soviet President Mikhail Gorbachev sent his condolences to Iran and the Soviet Red Cross sent a plane carrying rescue workers and supplies. [2] Trucks carrying aid also crossed into Iran from Soviet Azerbaijan. [3] Tass reported that a day of mourning had been declared in the mostly muslim republic and black ribbons hung from buildings in the capital city of Baku. [4] Aid began to arrive from other countries as the French relief team arrived Friday night. [5] The European Community granted $1.2 million in emergency aid and the U.S. sent $300,000 worth of relief supplies. [6] Pope John Paul II sent $100,000 from her personal fund.
