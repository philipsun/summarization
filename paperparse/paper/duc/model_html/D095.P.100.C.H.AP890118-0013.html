[1] Patrick E. Purdy, also known as Patrick West, was a blond, blue-eyed, 26-year-old drifter with a long criminal record in the LA area. [2] He lived with an aunt and uncle in Sandy, Oregon, from July to October, then traveled to Texas, Tennessee, Connecticut, and finally Lodi, California, near his hometown of Stockton, looking for work. [3] On Tuesday he opened fire on Cleveland Elementary School in Stockton, killing five children and wounding 29 others and a teacher, before taking his own life. [4] His aunt and grandmother described him as a nice young man but a loner and childhood alcoholic. [5] His father died years ago after being struck by a car.
