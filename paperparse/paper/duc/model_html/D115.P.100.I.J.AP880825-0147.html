[1] The families of Peace Corps volunteers Peter Giaquinta and Bettyjean Bouffard learned Thursday that they were safe. [2] Both Peter and Bettyjean live in area of Nepal hardest hit by the earthquake that killed over 750 in that country and India. [3] Peace Corps spokesman Jim Flanigan said all but three of the 157 volunteers in Nepal are safe. [4] The three still unaccounted for, whose names were not released, are from Michigan, Wisconsin and Pennsylvania. [5] Washed out roads, he explained, have compounded the difficulty of reaching volunteers in remote areas. [6] There are no Peace Corps workers in India.
