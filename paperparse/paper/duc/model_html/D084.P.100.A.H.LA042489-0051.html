[1] The battleship Iowa was welcomed back to Norfolk Naval Base on Sunday by 3000 silent family and friends. [2] Sailors with black arm bands lined the rails. [3] Two of the 11 crew members who survived the explosion that killed 47 stood on the blackened No.2 turret, with full crews on the other two. [4] Media were kept from families and crew. [5] A video of the explosion was released on Sunday. [6] The still-open gun breech, and flame and gases reaching lower decks, suggest the explosion occurred while powder was in transit from a powder hoist to the gun. [7] The four battleships will stay in service.
