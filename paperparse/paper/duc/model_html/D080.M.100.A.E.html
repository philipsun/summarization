[1] The McMartin Pre-School child molestation trial, the longest criminal trial in U.S. history, ended with a verdict of innocent for Raymond Buckey and is mother, Peggy McMartin Buckey. [2] The jury found the Buckeys innocent on 52 of the charges but deadlocked on another 13, causing a mistrial on those charges. [3] Prosecutors must now decide if they want a retrial on the deadlocked charges. [4] Originally involving 7 defendants, including Raymond Buckeys sister and grandmother, the charges against the sister and grandmother as well as three employees of the school were dropped. [5] Parents of the children involved expressed anger and disappoint at the verdict.
