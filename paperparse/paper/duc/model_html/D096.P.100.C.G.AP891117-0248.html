[1] The nation's two largest brewers, Anheuser Busch Cos., Inc. and Miller Brewing Co., are gearing up to sell their beer by staging mock football games in television commercials in conjunction with Super Bowl XXIV. [2] Attempting to take advantage of weeks of hype, both companies will spend upwards of $7 million. [3] Anheuser Busch's "Bud Bowl II" ad campaign will feature animated teams of Budweiser and Bud Light beer bottles rattling around on a pint-sized football field. [4] Miller's 12-week pre-Super Bowl campaign will center on a football game between a "Tastes Great" team and a "Less Filling" squad featuring NFL players.
