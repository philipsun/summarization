[1] Five children were slain in the schoolyard of Cleveland Elementary School on Tuesday, the victim of a drifter with a troubled past. [2] Patrick Edward Purdy, armed with an AK-47 and a pistol, entered the crowded schoolyard just before noon and opened fire. [3] When the shooting was over, five students lay dead and 29 others, as well as one teacher, were wounded. [4] Purdy lay dead of a self-inflicted gunshot wound to the head. [5] Cleveland Elementary School is two-thirds Asian and the five students, one Vietnamese and four Cambodian, as well as 19 of the wounded were all Asian.
