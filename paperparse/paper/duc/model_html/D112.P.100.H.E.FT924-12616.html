[1] The late Robert Maxwell was a strong supporter of chess and a chess set was always set up in his office. [2] Malcolm Pein, master chess player and director of chess and bridge, told of Maxwell's support of the game when he sponsored a section of the World Chess championships in New York. [3] Maxwell kept the chess and bridge publishing businesses when Pergamon sold to Elsevier last year. [4] Although it has taken a year to separate Chess Monthly and Bridge Monthly from the Maxwell empire, Chess and Bridge has not moved to new premises in London.
