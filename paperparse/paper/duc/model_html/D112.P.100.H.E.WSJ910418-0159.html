[1] British publisher Robert Maxwell plans to sell 45 to 49 percent of its shares in Mirror Group Ltd. in Britain. [2] The sale to investors in Britain and abroad is intended to raise around $448 million. [3] The announcement of the sale follows Maxwell's takeover of the unprofitable New York Daily News. [4] Maxwell had already sold Pergamon Press in another money raising move last month. [5] In other changes, Maxwell's son, Ian, will resign his post as managing director at Maxwell Communication to become deputy chairman of Mirror group. [6] Ian's brother Kevin will succeed him as managing director at Maxwell Communication.
