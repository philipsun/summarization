[1] The Senate Armed Services Committee has put the nomination of former Texas Senator John Tower on hold as it investigates Tower's use of alcohol and his ties to defense contractors. [2] This comes amid charges that the FBI overlooked some incidents in Tower's background. [3] Specifically, the FBI failed to investigate the circumstances of a blaze in Tower's hotel suite in Dallas in 1967 as well as investigating police records on several car accidents. [4] In one of these accidents, Tower slightly injured the son of Senator Joe Montoya. [5] Tower is also accused of giving contradictory testimony to the committee on his dealings with defense contractors.
