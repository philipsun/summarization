[1] A flash flood raged through small town areas along the Ohio River in eastern Ohio. [2] So far, at least ten people are known dead but dozens are missing. [3] The flood created a wall of water 15 to 20 feet high as it tore through the area. [4] The flood struck near the village of Shadyside, Ohio. [5] The village is about ten miles south of Wheeling, West Virginia. [6] A crowded bar in Shadyside was washed away when the torrent of water caved in a wall. [7] Only two people have been accounted for. [8] Ohio Governor Celeste declared a state of emergency after surveying the area.
