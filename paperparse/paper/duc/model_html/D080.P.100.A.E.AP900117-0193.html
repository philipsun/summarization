[1] Jurors in the McMartin pre-school trial remained deadlocked on 13 of the charges brought against Raymond Buckey and his mother, Peggy McMartin Buskey. [2] The decisions on the 52 charges in which they have reached a verdict will remain sealed until a decision is reached on how to proceed on the deadlocked charges. [3] Jurors were confused on some points of law in the deadlocked charges, especially on how to reach a verdict on the charge of conspiring between the two defendants; failing to see how they could find one, but not both, innocent of the charge. [4] Jurors are in their ninth week of deliberation.
