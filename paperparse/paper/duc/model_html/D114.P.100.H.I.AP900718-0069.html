[1] The Indian government today imposed direct federal rule over the troubled state of Jammu-Kashmir. [2] Government forces have battled Moslem separatists here since January, and now consider the state too unstable for local government. [3] Tuesday, 29 separatists were killed, bringing the death toll to at least 792. [4] Jammu-Kashmir is 65percent Moslem in the 82 percent-Hindu India, and its Moslems want to form an independent nation. [5] India is claiming Pakistan is arming and training the separatists. [6] India and Pakistan have already fought two wars over the area and talks between the two countries began today in Islamabad to try to mend current relations.
