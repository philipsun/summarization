[1] As recent actions by East Germany make German reunification more likely, many policy wonks point out serious problems to be overcome. [2] A new structure or relationship between NATO and the Warsaw Pact may be needed. [3] Theodore Sorenson, a top aide to JFK, states that the U.S. couldn't afford a united Germany outside the Western Alliance and the Soviets can't afford one inside the Western Alliance. [4] He hopes unification can be forged within a reunited Europe.
