[1] Former Senator John Tower, President Bush's nominee as defense secretary under went surgery to remove a colon polyp on Thursday. [2] Senator Tower was listed in fair condition following the surgery and is expected to make a full recovery. [3] Tower entered the hospital on Wednesday after a colonoscopy revealed the polyp. [4] A preliminary biopsy of the polyp, which was removed during a three hour surgery, showed it to be benign. [5] A rectal polyp was removed during the colonoscopy which showed malignant but well differentiated cells. [6] An announcement on the first polyp was to come from Tower's surgeon on Thursday.
