[1] The recent tragic sinking of the ferry Estonia, with the loss of 800-plus lives, has concentrated attention on making ferries safer. [2] Over the past two decades the United Nations Maritime Organization has devoted much thought to ship safety, but now in September 1994 it is an imperative. [3] Any review of ferry safety will focus on ferry design, enforcement of existing regulations, and crew training.
