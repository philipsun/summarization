[1] One of the three owners of Cory, a champion German short-haired pointer is suing the other two for damages and to determine if Cory is officially retired. [2] Cory has won 245 shows as Best-of-Breed and 19 awards as Best-in-Show. [3] Plaintiff Attalla claims the owners' agreement specifies that he advances fees to show the dog and he decides when Cory retires. [4] He is suing the other two owners, John and Rita Remondi for $125,000 in fees and to stop them from showing Cory, who has been officially retired as announced in Dog News. [5] The Remondis claim that Cory's retirement is fraudulent.
