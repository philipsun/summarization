[1] Lucille Ball was mourned in her hometown of Jamestown New York. [2] The 77-year-old comedian died of a ruptured aorta in Los Angeles 26 April 1989. [3] She was born in Jamestown in 1911 where she showed an early interest in show business. [4] She moved to Hollywood in 1933. [5] Although she had not visited Jamestown since 1956, she regularly donated money to charities, civic events and Jamestown community college. [6] She was to have received an honorary degree from the college in May. [7] The degree will be awarded posthumously. [8] Townspeople remembered her as full of life and expressed sadness at her passing.
