[1] The Booker Prize has become internationally known, and winning the prize is a boon to the author and publisher. [2] The 1990 winner will be announced on 16 October, and a worldwide boost of sales of the winning novel will follow. [3] Each British publishing house can submit for consideration up to three novels by British or Commonwealth authors. [4] A management committee selects a well-qualified panel of judges who meet and select the six finalists. [5] Interest builds and bookmakers establish odds on each author's chance of winning. [6] The judges announce the winner in the afternoon of announcement day.
