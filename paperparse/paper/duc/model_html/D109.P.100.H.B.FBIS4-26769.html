[1] Floods that hit Guangdong, Guangxi, Hunan, Jiangxi, Fujian and Zhejiang Provinces have all receded. [2] Caused by torrential rains in the Xijiang and Beijiang river valleys, the floods were the worst in 100 years. [3] The Guangdong provincial government urged localities to help victims with food, clothing, shelter and medical care so that no one would die and no epidemic disease would break out. [4] The province has allocated grain, cash and farm production supplies such as fertilizer and diesel fuel to the flood-stricken areas. [5] Central Party member Wen Jiabao said the key to mitigating economic loss is to resume production as soon as possible.
