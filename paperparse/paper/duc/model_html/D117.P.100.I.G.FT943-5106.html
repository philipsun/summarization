[1] The shortlist of six for the #20,000 Booker Prize for fiction prompted the publishing industry to comment that some on the list "are B-team writers at best" and that others "don't make the grade". [2] This is surprising commentary given it is a bumper year for new fiction fulfilling the criteria--English language and non-American. [3] There was some traditional controversy when it was revealed that an author whose novel had made the longlist was actually the wife of a Booker judge. [4] But critics say it's the final list, not the controversy, which must have a final death blow to the Booker.
