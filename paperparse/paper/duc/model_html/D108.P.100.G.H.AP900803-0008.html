[1] Saddam Hussain accused Kuwait and the UAE of flooding the oil market and driving prices down, costing Iraq $14 billion; Kuwait of stealing oil from Iraqi border wells; and Persian Gulf Arab states of conspiring with the US and Israel to weaken Iraq. [2] Kuwait suggested Iraq wanted to eliminate Iran-Iraq War debts. [3] The US announced US-UAE exercises in the Persian Gulf July 24th. [4] Kuwait and the UAE agreed to a halt in overproduction. [5] By July 31st, Iraq had 100,000 troops on Kuwait's border. [6] Iraq pulled out of talks with Kuwait in Jidda August 1st. [7] Iraqi tank-led troops invaded Kuwait at 2am August 2nd, and seized the palace and government buildings.
