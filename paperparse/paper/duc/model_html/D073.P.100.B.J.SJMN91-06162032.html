[1] Clark AFB, Philippines, was evacuated today after gases, ash and rock spewed from Mount Pinatubo located 10 miles from the U.S. base. [2] Philippine Institute of Vulcanology and Seismology Director Raymundo Punongbayan warned that bigger eruptions would follow. [3] Subic Bay naval base will house the nearly 15,000 evacuees. [4] Fifteen hundred troops remained at Clark to provide security. [5] Since Pinatubo began erupting last week, Philippine officials have evacuated more than 12,000 people in three provinces. [6] Pinatubo is in the "Ring of Fire" that circles the Pacific. [7] Mount Unzen in Japan, also in the ring, erupted a week ago, killing 38 people.
