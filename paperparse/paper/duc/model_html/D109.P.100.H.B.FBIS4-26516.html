[1] Floods lasting for ten or more days have brought heavy agricultural losses to Fujian Province. [2] Among nine cities and prefectures there, those seriously hit are Sanming, Nanping, Ningde, Fuzhou, Quanzhou and Putian. [3] Of these, Sanming was hit the worst. [4] In May the city suffered from heavy rainstorms and had big losses and it now suffers again from the flood with some 600,000 mu of crops damaged. [5] Per capita possession of cultivated land in Fujian is only 0.05 mu. [6] Even in normal years, grain output there is not self-sufficient. [7] This consecutive flooding has made things even more grim.
