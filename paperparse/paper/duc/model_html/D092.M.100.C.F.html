[1] The largest earthquake (magnitude 7.7) ever to strike Iran occurred on June 20th. [2] The Islamic Republic put the death toll at 36,000 and the number homeless at 400,000. [3] The opposition People's Mojahedin blamed the government for shoddy housing that caused the deaths, which they said were underreported. [4] The government, in turn, blamed the US for robbing it of oil resources and leaving too little for developing modern construction methods. [5] Iran said it would accept aid from all countries, even the US, but not from Israel and South Africa. [6] Relief supplies poured into Iran from all over the world, including the US.
