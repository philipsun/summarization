[1] The World Jewish Congress plans to hold a conference at Wansee where the Nazi leadership met on January 20, 1942 to implement the "final solution" of the Jewish problem. [2] The meeting is scheduled for May 8th, the 45th anniversary of VE day. [3] It will be the first meeting on German soil, and 100 are expected to attend. [4] The President of the organization says that Jews must make a moral statement on German reunification, and Wansee is the right place to make it. [5] Part of the statement is that the Holocaust must never be repeated.
