[1] Paul Weyrich, president of the conservative Free Congress Foundation testified at the Senate Armed Services Committee that John Tower's behavior makes him unfit to lead the Pentagon. [2] Weyrich says that Tower may lack the "moral character" to be secretary of defense and cites lack of sobriety and womanizing. [3] Before this testimony, members of the committee and witnesses had praise for the former senator. [4] Other testimony raised questions regarding Tower's lucrative ties to defense contractors and past support for big defense budgets. [5] Senators Nunn and Warner have asked the White House if allegations against Tower have been investigated by the FBI.
