[1] After inspecting Zhuzhou and Xiangtan flood areas, Comrade Wen Jiabao (CPC Central Committee Secretariat member) commended the work in the two cities and issued important instructions. [2] Under the leadership of the provincial party committee and government, the cadres and people had rescued a number of industrial and mining enterprises. [3] After the water recedes, they should concentrate on aiding the victims, resuming production and reconstructing homes. [4] He noted that the peak flood season has not arrived, and the people cannot relax. [5] He also stressed that the current flooding has revealed problems in the construction of water conservancy facilities and urban flood control.
