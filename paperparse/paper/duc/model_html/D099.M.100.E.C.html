[1] Marathons are contested over a course that measures 26 miles and 385 yards. [2] They are very popular, and most of the world's major cities organize an annual marathon.. [3] Tour companies even put together "marathon tours" to accommodate runners. [4] Most marathons offer substantial prize money to race winners, but most in the throngs of participants run only for a sense of accomplishment. [5] Each city tailors its marathon to provide the best of its natural beauty and history. [6] London has the largest, and Boston the most storied. [7] San Francisco and San Diego boast of their weather conditions.
