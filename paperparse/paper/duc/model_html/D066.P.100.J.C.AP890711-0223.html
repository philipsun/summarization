[1] The 1989 publication of the Forbes listing of the richest people again has Sam Walton as the nation's richest man. [2] He has been on top since Forbes first published the listing in 1988. [3] This year he is in the list of the wealthiest in the world. [4] The annual Forbes publication makes Walton uncomfortable. [5] He shuns the personal publicity that goes with his status, preferring to concentrate on Wal-Mart and pursue the simple life on Bentonville, Arkansas. [6] He calls his salespeople "associates", and frequently visits them at one of the stores.
