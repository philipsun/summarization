[1] Guangdong Province Governor Zhu Senlin, inspecting flood control and relief work in Qingyuan city yesterday, said the Beijiang and Xijiang rivers were receding from their record levels. [2] He attributed the province's success in combating the flood to the concerted efforts of locally stationed soldiers stationed and residents. [3] More than 200 people died, 189,000 rooms were destroyed, and 1.2 million hectares of crops ruined in flooding due to successive torrential rainstorms in the river valleys this month. [4] The economic losses were set at 10.2 billion yuan. [5] The reservoirs, however, remained intact and Guangzhou, the capital city, and Zhujiang River Delta remained safe.
