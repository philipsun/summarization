[1] Julia Baird, former Beatle John Lennon's half-sister, has written a memoir about Lennon's younger years. [2] Baird wrote the book in response to the portrayal of John Lennon as moody neglected child. [3] Baird feels that their mother has been maligned and decided to write the book after seeing a television show that presented her mother as an "unfit mother, no mother at all". [4] Baird was born seven years after Lennon and she and her sister grew up with their parents amid an extended family, including brother John. [5] She writes that her mother and Lennon had a loving relationship, even though living apart.
