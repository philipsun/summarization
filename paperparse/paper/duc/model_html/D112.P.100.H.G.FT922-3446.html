[1] Two Swiss trusts, a private UK trust company and a US investment bank are under investigation by the Serious Fraud Office (SFO) for involvement in an illegal scheme to support the Maxwell Communication Corporation (MCC) share price. [2] Robert Maxwell and his son Kevin, the owners of MCC, are being investigated by SFO for failure to disclose purchases of shares by Swiss Trusts as required by company law. [3] A directive of a secretive Swiss trust said the purchases, using brokers, had been "going on for some time" and were bought for other "foundations" and then held for a certain time.
