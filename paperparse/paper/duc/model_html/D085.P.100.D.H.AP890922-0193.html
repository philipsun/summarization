[1] Hurricane Hugo started as a tropical wave off Africa on Sept. 11th, was named tropical storm Hugo with 39mph winds on Sep 12th, and became a hurricane with 74mph winds on Sep 13th. [2] On Sep 15th it was designated Category 4 with 150mph winds. [3] On Sep 17th it damaged Guadeloupe, Montserrat, Antigua, St. Kitts, and the Virgin Islands, where St. Thomas and St. Croix were worst hit. [4] On Sep 18th it damaged Puerto Rico. [5] Winds slowed to 105 mph then returned to 135mph and Category 4 designation on the 21st, when it hit Charleston. [6] It continued inland with diminishing winds on the 22nd and was downgraded to a tropical storm.
