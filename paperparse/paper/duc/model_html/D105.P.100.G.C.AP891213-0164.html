[1] The Tatars of the Crimea and the ethnic Germans who lived along the Volga were forcibly resettled by Stalin. [2] They were sent to Siberia and Central Asia, and now they want to return to their traditional homeland. [3] The government has decided to give each of each of the groups an autonomous region in their homeland, but it will be years before they can be relocated. [4] The Russians who have settled in those areas, and wish to remain there must be accommodated before the Tatars and the Germans can return to the Volga.
