[1] The San Francisco 49ers routed the Denver Broncos 55-10 Sunday in the most lopsided Super Bowl victory ever. [2] This was the 49ers fourth win in four tries, tying them with the Pittsburgh Steelers for the NFL championship. [3] San Francisco quarterback Joe Montana set several Super Bowl records; five touchdown passes; 13 straight pass completions; a third Super Bowl MVP award. [4] San Francisco's point total was the most ever. [5] In four Super Bowls, Montana has thrown 11 touchdowns and no interceptions. [6] It was a day of futility for Broncos' quarterback John Elway as his team suffered its third Super Bowl defeat.
