[1] The McMartin Pre-School molestation case began in August 1983 when Judy Johnson accused Raymond Buckey of molesting her son. [2] By March 1984, a public outcry had been created and seven people were arrested for abusing 18 children over 10 years. [3] In January 1986, the preliminary hearing, after 18 months and $4 million, brought 135 counts against the seven. [4] Charges later were dismissed against all but Buckey and his mother. [5] Jury selection began in April 1987 and the actual trial ran from July until November 1989. [6] On January 18, 1990 they were acquitted on 52 counts, with a mistrial declared on 13 counts.
