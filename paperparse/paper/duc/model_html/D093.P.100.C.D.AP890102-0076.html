[1] A passenger ferry sank off the coast of Guatemala while being towed to port. [2] Fifty-nine people drowned and 6 are missing. [3] The ferry ran out of fuel mid-way on a regular 16- mile run across Amatique Bay and sank while a tugboat was towing it to shore. [4] A Spanish citizen was reported dead, but his wife and son survived. [5] The victim was working with a Spanish technical aid and economic development mission in Guatemala. [6] The nationality of the other victims was not known. [7] Navy patrol boats, fishing vessels and private citizens have been searching for survivors and recovering bodies.
