[1] Philippine President Corazon Aquino is in West Germany today seeking expanded economic ties. [2] She compared the Philippines' economic situation with that of Germany following World War II and asked Germany to share its experiences. [3] She projected the debt to increase to $29 billion by December. [4] She said the nation's growth rate changed from minus 10 percent in 1985 to plus 6.7 percent in 1988. [5] Debt relief and investments are her goal. [6] German leaders promised strengthened relations and continuing trade in the future European Economic Community market. [7] Aquino is on the first stop of a European tour to include Paris and Belgium.
