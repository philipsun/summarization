[1] The owners of Cory, a German short-haired pointer are embroiled in a legal battle over whether or not he should be retired. [2] Cory has won 245 show awards as Best of Breed and 19 awards as Best in Show. [3] One owner, Anthony Attalla is suing the other two, John and Rita Renmondi, charging that they have been showing Cory even though he is officially retired. [4] Attalla claims that as he lays out fees and other expenses, it is his decision when to retire Cory. [5] The Renmondi's claim that Attalla has breached the contract by not paying certain expenses. [6] Stud fees are also at issue.
