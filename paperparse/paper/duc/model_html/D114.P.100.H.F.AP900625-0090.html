[1] The Hezbul Mujahedeen is believed responsible for kidnapping and killing four Moslems whose bodies were found in the town of Sopore. [2] The Hezbul Mujahedeen is one of more than a dozen Moslem organizations fighting for independence of the Kashmir valley from India. [3] The murdered Moslems were said to be police informants. [4] A militant ambush of an army convoy killed one soldier and wounded three others. [5] These deaths raise the total of those killed since January 20th to 630. [6] Moslem militants had earlier demanded union with Pakistan, but more recently are demanding independence for Kashmir and the status of a neutral nation.
