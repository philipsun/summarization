[1] The 50th anniversary of John Lennon's birth was observed in a global celebration. [2] Following a brief ceremony at he United Nations, Lennon's peace anthem "Imagine" was played for an estimated one billion people in 130 nations. [3] More than 1,000 radio stations and networks from 50 countries had signed on for the satellite broadcast. [4] Fans gathered in Hollywood at the late Beatle's sidewalk star. [5] In Liverpool, Lennon's hometown, fans and civic dignitaries paid tribute. [6] Fans in New York planned a pilgrimage to the spot outside the Dakota apartment building where Lennon was murdered nearly 10 years ago.
