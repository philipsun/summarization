[1] A column by Fahd al-Fanik in Arabic in the Amman AL-RA'Y, notes that the new US ambassador to Jordan is a heavyweight having served in Cairo, Saudi Arabia, two African countries, and a European country. [2] Wesley Egan, the new ambassador, is expected to pay special attention to stability in Jordan and its economic development. [3] However, most of al-Fanik's column is aimed at the comments of Senator Daniel Moynihan at Egan's confirmation hearing. [4] The writer calls the senator the "Zionist Democrat from New York". [5] Moynihan's saying the West Bank and Gaza are inappropriately called occupied territories, the writer says, mirrors the Zionist lobby in the US.
