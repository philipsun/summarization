[1] Andrei Sakharov, Soviet hydrogen bomb developer and prominent 1970s dissident, won the 1975 Nobel Peace Prize. [2] He was exiled to Gorky from 1980-1986. [3] Sakharov supported Gorbachev's reforms but urged improved human rights. [4] In April 1989 he was elected to parliament. [5] He died in December 1989, aged 68, of an apparent heart attack.
