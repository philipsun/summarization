[1] Dave Johnson of the United States clinched the decathlon gold medal in the final event at the World University Games on Sunday. [2] Johnson trailed Mikhail Medved of the Soviet Union by 11 points before the 1,500-meter race, the final event of the two-day competition. [3] In some other events: Julius Kariuki of Kenya won the men's 10,000-meter race; Romania won four golds in rowing and two in track and field; Reggie Davis won the gold for the U.S. in the 400-meter hurdles; Ana Quirot of Cuba won the women's 400-meter race and Jaime Jefferson of Cuba won the men's long jump.
