[1] Authorities charges a ferry captain with negligent homicide after the boat, overcrowded with New Year's travelers sank off Guatemala' east coast. [2] Sixty-seven people drowned, 14 were missing and 46 survived. [3] The ferry ran out of fuel mid-way in its journey and sank after a naval patrol boat tried to tow it to port. [4] The ferry captain was slow to respond to requests by the skipper of the naval patrol boat to take control of the ferry's wheel once it was under tow. [5] The ferry captain radioed that he had taken the wheel just as the boat began to capsize.
