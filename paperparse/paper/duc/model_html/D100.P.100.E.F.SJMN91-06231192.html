[1] Yoko Ono, widow of the late Beatle, John Lennon promotes her own works, but her main activity is to promote Lennon's art and image. [2] She and Lennon were married in 1970. [3] Some believe she controlled him but others question if he could be controlled. [4] She is often blamed for the breakup of the Beatles, but that would likely have happened without her. [5] The remaining ex-Beatles were never fond of her. [6] Ono was born in Tokyo but lived in the US from 1936 until WWII, when the family had to return to Japan. [7] She had two other marriages before Lennon. [8] She now lives in New York and Geneva.
