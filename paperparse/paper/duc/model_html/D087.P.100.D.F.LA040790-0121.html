[1] Milan Bakes, a lieutenant in the Czech army set a world record in the kneeling portion of the three position rifle competition en route to a gold medal at the 1990 World Cup USA Olympic-style shooting tournament in Chino, California. [2] Francoise Decharne of France won the women's air rifle competition in the most closely contested event of the day.
