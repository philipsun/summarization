[1] This is a viewer's guide to Super Bowl XXV. [2] It will be played at Tampa Stadium, Tampa Fla. [3] Winners will get $36,000 per man; losers get $18,000 per man. [4] If the game is tied at the end of regulation time, 60 minutes, it will continue in sudden death overtime. [5] The team scoring first will win. [6] The winning team receives permanent possession of the Vince Lombardi Trophy, a sterling silver football mounted in a kicking position that was created by Tiffany and is valued at over $10,000. [7] The late Vince Lombardi coached the two-time Super Bowl champion Green Bay Packers.
