[1] Lucille Ball who died 26 April, 1989 at the age of 77, was remembered in her hometown of Jamestown, New York. [2] Miss Ball was born in Jamestown in 1911 and regularly donated money to charities, civic events and Jamestown Community college. [3] Although the comedian had not visited Jamestown since 1956 she kept in touch with friends there. [4] They describe her as interested in show business from an early age and though a big star, just "Lucy" to her friends. [5] She had planned to return to Jamestown in May to receive an honorary degree from the college. [6] They now plan to award it posthumously.
