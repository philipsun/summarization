[1] A freighter with a cargo of beer sank today 28 nautical miles north of the Ameland, a Dutch island in the North Sea. [2] This is an area of heavy freight traffic. [3] The ship sank about 12:45 P.M. in high winds. [4] A West German helicopter from the island of Borkum, 30 miles from where the ship went down, took the crewmen ashore. [5] The Coast Guard identified the ship as a Finnish-registered freighter, but did not identify it by name. [6] The ship's origin and destination were not provided. [7] Earlier reports had identified the ship as a passenger ferry.
