[1] The Leonard Bernstein Gala Birthday Performance is a benefit concert scheduled for the composer's 70th birthday, August 25, to raise money for the Tanglewood Music Center, where Bernstein started his conducting career. [2] Beverly Sills will host. [3] Lauren Bacall, Betty Comden, and Phyllis Newman are among the performers who will sing, act, and make guest appearances. [4] The Boston Symphony Orchestra, the Boston Pops Orchestra, and the Tanglewood Festival Chorus, directed by some of the conductors mentored by Bernstein, will also perform. [5] The concert will celebrate Bernstein's contributions to classical and popular compositions and landmark opera recordings. [6] Tickets cost $20 to $5000.
