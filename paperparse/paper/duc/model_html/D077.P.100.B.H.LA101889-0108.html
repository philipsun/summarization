[1] A reporter at Candlestick Park Tuesday night when the earthquake hit first thought a low- flying jet had passed overhead. [2] The stadium shook and the special concrete joints strategically spaced around the top of the stadium to prevent earthquake damage did what they were designed to, opening and closing as if the stadium was cardboard instead of concrete.
