[1] The annual Academy Awards ceremony has generated a wealth of statistics and trivia in its 60 years. [2] Oscar historians chronicle what actors, directors, studios, and films have received the most Oscars or been nominated most often. [3] Other factoids concern the youngest, oldest, or posthumous recipients; family members who have been recipients in the same picture; actors nominated in multiple categories, performers who have refused their Oscars, those who have never appeared to collect their award; the number of TV viewers, the number of countries watching, the speed of reading the rules, and who decapitated his Oscar while practicing his golf swing.
