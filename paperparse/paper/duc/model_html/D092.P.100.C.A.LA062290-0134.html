[1] Iranian news media reacted sluggishly Thursday to an earthquake that left 25,000 dead in northern Iran. [2] For hours the only detailed reporting by the Islamic Republic News Agency (IRNA) was that Tehran residents were either asleep or watching a World Cup soccer match when the quake hit. [3] IRNA also underestimated the severity of the quake reporting 10,000 dead late Thursday while the Iranian ambassador to the United Nations said 25,000 had died. [4] Official radio was off the air when the quake hit and only reported the earthquake 6.5 hours later. [5] Like IRNA radio reporting lacked color and description for hours.
