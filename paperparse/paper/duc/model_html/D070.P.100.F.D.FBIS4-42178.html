[1] Erich Honecker, the former head of East Germany, died on 29 May 1994 in Santiago Chile. [2] The 81-year-old Honecker and his wife Margot Honecker, had been living with their daughter in Santiago, since his release from prison in 1993. [3] Honecker had been suffering from liver cancer. [4] Honecker's lawyer, Nicolas Becker said that while he had refused an operation, Honecker had done everything sensible in regard to treatment. [5] It is not clear whether or not Margot Honecker will be allowed to remain in Chile. [6] It was not known if Honecker had left a will or where the funeral would be held.
