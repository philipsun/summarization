[1] Hurricane Gilbert brought more than 24 hours of heavy rain to Monterrey and Matamoros, Mexico, causing severe flooding and deaths. [2] In the industrial city of Monterrey, at least 10 police officers on a rescue mission were swept away by the Santa Catarina River.
