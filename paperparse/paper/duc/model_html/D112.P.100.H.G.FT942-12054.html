[1] Trustees filed two writs last week in Manhattan for Mirror Group Newspaper Pension Scheme and Maxwell Communications Corporation Works Pension Scheme. [2] The writs allege that Goldman Sachs, the US-based investment bank, assisted in diverting currency from the two Robert Maxwell-controlled pension schemes to ensure its own debts from Maxwell interest would be repaid. [3] The writs center on the purchase of #55 million of MCC shares from the pension schemes of the two Swiss trusts which were transacted through Goldman. [4] Allegedly, Kevin Maxwell told Goldman to remit the proceeds of the sale to a trust Goldman knew to be Maxwell owned.
