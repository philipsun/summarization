[1] Dave Johnson of the US clinched the decathlon gold medal in the final event of the World University Games. [2] Another American, Sheldon Blockburger, was in third place in the decathlon until that last event but was overtaken by Szabo of Hungary. [3] Medved of the USSR won the silver. [4] Americans won gold and bronze in the men's 400-meter hurdles. [5] Ana Quirot of Cuba beat Jearl Miles of the US in the women's 400 meters. [6] American Llewellyn Starks was third in the long jump. [7] Romanian Paula Ivan set a meet record in winning the women's 3,000-meter race.
