[1] Comrade Wen Jiabao and his party left Changsha yesterday to inspect the flooded areas of Zhuzhou and Xiangtan. [2] After listening to these two cities' flood fighting and relief reports, he spoke out. [3] He said that by their work, the cadres and people in Zhuzhou and Xiangtan rescued a large number of industrial and mining enterprises. [4] After the water recedes we should rebuild homes and repair the damaged dikes and embankments. [5] Since the peak flood season has not yet arrived we cannot slacken our efforts. [6] There has been inadequate coordination between urban and rural flood control. [7] We should learn from this negative experience.
