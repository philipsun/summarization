[1] Cynthia Lennon, former wife of the late John Lennon, joined others in denouncing the unauthorized biography of the late, former Beatle. [2] Cynthia Lennon and Lennon's widow, Yoko Ono, both appeared on the CBS show "60 Minutes" in a segment called "The Two Mrs. [3] Lennons". Both had angry words for Albert Goldman, who authored the biography The Lives of John Lennon. [4] Cynthia's and John's son Julian called the book "lies, untruths". [5] Sean Lennon, the 12-year old son of John and Yoko rebutted the charge that his father was inattentive to parenting. [6] Lennon was murdered by Mark David Chapman outside his apartment building in 1980.
