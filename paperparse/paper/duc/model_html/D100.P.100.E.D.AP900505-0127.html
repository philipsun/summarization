[1] Turnout for a concert marking what would have been the 50th birthday of John Lennon was disappointing. [2] Organizers had hoped for 45,000 attendees, but only 15,000 came. [3] The concert featured a program of Beatles and Lennon songs. [4] Ex-Beatles did not attend, but Paul McCartney and Ringo Starr made taped contributions. [5] Proceeds of the concert will go to the Spirit Foundation, the charity founded by John Lennon and his wife Ono in 1978. [6] Lennon was slain on December 8, 1980 at the age of 40. [7] Mark Chapman, the man who killed him is serving a life sentence.
