[1] Ukrainian coal miners at the coal production association and at three pits, went on strike demanding payment of wage arrears. [2] A total of 1200 of the association's 2248 workers are on strike. [3] Workers at another mine refused to start work on their shifts until the mine's director assured them the arrears would be paid. [4] Some members are also demanding the dismissal of the association's director general. [5] In the opinion of one mine's representative, the strike has a clear political hue to compromise the existing authorities, particularly President Kravchuk, before the second round of presidential elections.
