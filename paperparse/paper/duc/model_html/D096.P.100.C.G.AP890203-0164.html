[1] President Bush at an East Room reception congratulated the San Francisco 49ers on their 20-16 Super Bowl victory. [2] Surrounded by 49ers, Bush said they rewrote the record books and raised football to a new level of excellence. [3] Quarterback Joe Montana said being invited to the White House "meant a lot to us all... [4] Not very often do I get to see these guys in ties". [5] Bush was presented with autographed footballs by Montana and wide receiver Jerry Rice, who won the game's MVP award; a Super Bowl ring by team owner Danny DeBartolo; and a 49ers jogging outfit by Coach Bill Walsh.
