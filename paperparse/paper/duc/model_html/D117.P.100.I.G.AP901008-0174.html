[1] The Booker Prize, Britain's literary event of the year is guaranteed to boost sales of the chosen novel. [2] The winning author receives a check for #20,000 ($38,000) sponsored by Booker, an international food and agriculture business. [3] Book Trust, a charity promoting reading and books, administers the prize. [4] British publishers submit three new novels by British and Commonwealth writers. [5] A management committee of authors, booksellers, librarians, and the sponsors choose a jury of five: a writer and/or an academic, politician, editor or critic. [6] Each juror reads every book and meets with other jurists to select six finalists.
