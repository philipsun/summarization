[1] John Kelley is training for the 1992 Boston Marathon next April. [2] At 83 years of age he will be the oldest competitor, as he has been for 15 years. [3] Kelley won the marathon in 1935 and again in 1945, and expects to finish ahead of many of the 13,000 who will compete in April. [4] Each year Kelley visits the Cooper Clinic in Dallas where he undergoes a battery of tests. [5] The clinic gives a stress test and Kelley holds the endurance record for his age group. [6] Doctor Cooper says Kelley is a medical marvel.
