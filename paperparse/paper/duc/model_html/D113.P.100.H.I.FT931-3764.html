[1] More than a dozen bombs exploded yesterday afternoon in Bombay, killing about 200 and injuring more than a 1,000. [2] The devices, mostly car bombs, appeared to be a systematic attack on India's commercial heart. [3] Targets included the Bombay Stock Exchange, Air India building, a shopping complex, and two hotels near the airport. [4] The identity of the bombers is unknown, but the goal appears to be renewing communal strife and undermining government efforts to attract foreign investment and tourism. [5] Leaders have called for calm. [6] All roads into Bombay are blocked and the army is on alert.
