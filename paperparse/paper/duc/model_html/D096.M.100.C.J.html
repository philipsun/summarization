[1] Super Bowl XXIV gave the 49ers their fourth Vince Lombardi Trophy. [2] Sixty-three percent of TV viewers watched the lopsided game which also featured the conclusion of the "Bud Bowl II," a $7 million Anheuser Bush ad campaign. [3] Though the game can spotlight a community's problems, it also has tremendous economic impact. [4] Communities such as Los Angeles--Orange County, therefore, offer incentives, such as rent-free stadium use, to lure the game to their city. [5] Even with tougher business entertainment tax laws, companies are well represented at the event. [6] Traditionally, the winning Super Bowl team is invited to the White House.
