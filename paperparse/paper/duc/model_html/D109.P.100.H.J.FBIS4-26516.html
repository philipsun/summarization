[1] According to the Provincial Agricultural Bureau, Fujian suffered major losses due to flooding. [2] Of the 2.465 million mu of crops affected by the flood, 446,000 mu will have no yield and 1.957 million mu of grain have been heavily hit. [3] Sanming, Nanping, Ningde, Fuzhou, and Quanzhou were seriously hit by flooding, with Sanming being the most devastated. [4] In May, a heavy rainstorm caused major losses there. [5] With the recent flooding, an additional 600,000 mu of crops were damaged and 200 million kilogram of grain is expected to be lost. [6] The floods have exacerbated the typically low grain production situation in Fujian.
