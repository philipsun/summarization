[1] Ten years after his death, Yoko Ono, wife of former Beatle John Lennon is thriving. [2] The Whitney Museum of Art has exhibited her artwork, the American Film Festival is staging an international tour of her films and a new album is soon to be released. [3] She also founded Bag One Arts, to promote Lennon's art and image. [4] Ono, 57, began her art career as an abstract impressionist, but moved to avant-garde. [5] She met Lennon in 1966 and many have accused her of breaking up the Beatles. [6] Ono was born in Tokyo and moved to the United States in 1936.
