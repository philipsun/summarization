[1] Ousted East German leader Erich Honecker was arrested and taken to prison today and a prosecutor said he will be tried for high treason in March. [2] Honecker was detained after his release from hospital, where he had undergone surgery for a malignant kidney tumor. [3] The charge of high treason carries a maximum life imprisonment. [4] East Germany has been caught up in a virtual frenzy to root out corruption and abuse of office since Honecker's ouster in October. [5] Eleven members of Honecker's ousted Politburo already are in prison awaiting trial, however his doctor repeated that Honecker is still not well enough to be jailed.
