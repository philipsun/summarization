[1] Michail Gorbachev urged all sides to take their time in deciding the fate of Erich Honecker as former East German leader spent a seventh day of refuge inside the Chilean Embassy in Moscow. [2] Germany wants to try Honecker for ordering the shooting of people who attempted to flee to the West during his years in power. [3] But speculation lingers that German politicians have little interest in trying a man who may possess embarrassing secrets.
