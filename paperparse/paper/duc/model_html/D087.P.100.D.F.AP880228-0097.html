[1] The US had its worst Winter Olympic Games showing in 52 years, taking only six medals. [2] Two of the medals were gold won by Brian Boitano in figure skating and Bonnie Blair in speed skating. [3] Blair also won a bronze, making her the only US double winner. [4] The US narrowly missed winning a bronze in the bobsled event when they were beaten by only.02 seconds. [5] The Soviet hockey team had it first loss of the Games at the hands of Finland but still managed to win the gold while Finland won the Silver. [6] Overall, the Soviets won the most medals, followed by East Germany and Switzerland .
