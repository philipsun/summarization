[1] Iraqi President Saddam Hussain accused Kuwait and the UAE of flooding the oil market and driving prices down, costing Iraq $14 billion. [2] Iraq accused Kuwait of stealing oil from wells along the Iraqi border. [3] Kuwait suggested Iraq was trying to write off its Iran- Iraq War debts. [4] The US announced US-UAE exercises in the Persian Gulf July 24th. [5] Hussain refused to be intimidated and summoned the US ambassador for talks. [6] Kuwait and the UAE pledged to abide by an OPEC-agreed halt in overproduction. [7] By July 31st, Iraq had 100,000 troops on Kuwait's border. [8] Iraqi-Kuwaiti talks in Jidda collapsed August 1st. [9] Iraqi troops invaded Kuwait at 2am August 2nd.
