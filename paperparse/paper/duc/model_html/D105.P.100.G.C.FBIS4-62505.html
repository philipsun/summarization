[1] On 16 May 1994 the Tajik Communist Party called for the restoration of a rejuvenated Soviet Union in the form of a federation as the only way to restore order, and said a majority of Tajiks supported the call. [2] The Communist Party leader said that the disintegration of the Soviet Union had led to economic and political chaos in all the republics. [3] Last Saturday supporters of the restoration movement held a funding congress in Dushanbe, capital of Tajikistan. [4] Ethnic conflicts have disrupted many republics, but Tajikistan was hit the hardest, with 100,00 killed.
