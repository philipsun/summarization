[1] Iraqi troops, led by the elite Republican Guard invaded Kuwait early today as Iraq claimed it was responding to the request from "the interim government of free Kuwait" to defend the revolution and the Kuwaiti people. [2] Kuwaiti forces failed in their attempt to repel the invaders with heavy artillery. [3] The rattle of automatic weapons and explosions in rapid succession rudely awakened residents.
