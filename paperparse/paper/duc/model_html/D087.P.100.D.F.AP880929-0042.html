[1] Florence Griffith Joyner is on target to become only the second woman to win four gold medals in track and field events in a single Olympics. [2] Her gold medals were in the 100 and 200 meter sprints set new Olympic and World records. [3] The flamboyant Griffith Joyner is confident that she can run even faster. [4] Her next chance to win gold would come in the 400 and 1600 meter relays. [5] If successful in those events, she would match the quadruple gold medal performance by American Carl Lewis in 1984 and Fanny Blankers-Koen of the Netherlands in 1948.
