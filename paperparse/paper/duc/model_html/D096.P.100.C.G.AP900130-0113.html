[1] The San Francisco 49ers 55-10 rout of the Denver Broncos was the lowest-rated Super Bowl in 21 years and the third lowest ever since the 1969 and 1968 Super Bowls. [2] The game averaged a 39.0 rating and 63 share. [3] The rating is a percentage of the nation's televisions; each point represents 921,000 homes. [4] The share is the percentage of TVs on at the time. [5] Despite the low rating, about 108.5 million watched the game, making it the ninth most-watched TV show ever behind eight Super Bowls and the final episode of MASH. [6] Higher viewership was due to the annual increase in the number of homes with TV.
