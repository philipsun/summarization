[1] A team led by Wen Jiabao, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and member of the CPC Central Committee Secretariat, arrived in Jiangxi to provide on-the-spot guidance for the on-going flood fighting and relief work and to direct the resumption of industrial and agricultural production. [2] Wen extended appreciation to everyone who has been doing this emergency work and affirmed their preliminary victories. [3] He emphasized that measures should be taken to salvage the early-ripening rice and to expand the planting of late-ripening rice and autumn grain in order to attain a bumper crop for the entire year.
