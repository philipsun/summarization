[1] A State Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters spokesmen said today that floods along the Xijiang, Beijiang, Xiangjiang and Ganjiang have receded, except for a section of the Xijiang in Wuzhou. [2] Floods hit Guangdong and several other provinces following heavy June storms. [3] The provincial government yesterday urged localities to assist victims and has allocated grain, cash reserves and farm production supplies to the flood-stricken areas. [4] Additionally, 790 million yuan in loans and donations were sent to the disaster area. [5] CPC Central Committee Secretariat member Wen Jiabao inspected flood areas and stated production should be promptly resumed to mitigate economic losses.
